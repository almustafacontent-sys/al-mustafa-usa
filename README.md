# Al Mustafa Relief Aid USA

## Serving Humanity Through Transparency & Trust

Al Mustafa USA is a registered humanitarian organization committed to poverty relief, orphan sponsorship, clean water projects, emergency aid, and Zakat distribution.

We operate with transparency and accountability to ensure every donation reaches those who need it most.

---

## Our Core Programs

###  Orphan Sponsorship
Provide monthly support, education, healthcare, and stability to vulnerable children.

### Clean Water Projects
Build wells and water systems to deliver sustainable access to clean water.

### Zakat & Sadaqah Distribution
We follow a strict transparency policy ensuring 100% proper allocation of funds.

### Emergency Relief
Rapid response support for disasters, war zones, and crisis situations. our priority is to serve the kids of Gaza.
